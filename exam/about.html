Some things about me

My name is Diana Morariu, I am 19 years old and I come from Romania. Ever since I was little I wanted to study abroad, so here I am in Copenhagen, Denmark. I study Multimedia Design and Technology at Copenhagen School of Design and Technology. I am a very curious person so I love learning new things and meeting new people and cultures. Usually I am happy and I like to lighten up the atmosphere in any way I can. I am pretty optimistic and I always try to help people even as little as possible. I am mostly interested in Graphic Design, but I also like programming. Drawing, reading and doing sports are my hobbies.
